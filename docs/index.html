WHAT SETS US APART

BETTER FOR A REASON

Direct Trade

Our coffee travels straight from the growers to the roasting pot. Direct trade is so amazing because it allows for mutually beneficial and respectful relationships with individual producers and cooperatives. This ensures the highest levels of quality and helps more people around the world earn a competitive wage.
Micro-Roasted

Our Coffee is micro-roasted, which is considered by many to be the best-tasting coffee available on the retail market. The processes used to create this variety are calculated to bring out the best of the coffee beans. This type of coffee has a richness of flavor unmatched by many traditional, store-bought brands.
Made in the USA

Obviously the best coffee beans are not grown in the USA. But after being purchased from the growers, our beans are expertly roasted and lovingly packaged right here in the United States. Oh, and those premium, valved, foil bags... fully made in the USA.
